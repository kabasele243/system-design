## Key Observations for Teaching

### Optimal Teaching Strategy
1. **Start with analogy** (Coffee shop, ATM, etc.)
2. **Present scenario** (Instagram needs to handle 1B users)
3. **Ask guiding questions** (What would you choose?)
4. **Challenge reasoning** (But what about mobile users?)
5. **Provide complete explanation** with real-world examples
6. **Test understanding** with practice questions
7. **Allow mastery** before moving to next concept

### Response Patterns
- **When confident**: Provides detailed, structured answers with reasoning
- **When uncertain**: Honestly says "I don't know" rather than guessing
- **When stuck**: Asks to restart or backtrack for clarity
- **When engaged**: Thinks beyond the question, considers edge cases
